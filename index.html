LifeScope is a comprehensive health management platform that facilitates seamless interaction among patients, physicians, and pharmacists. Key features include patient health tracking, appointment scheduling, vitals monitoring, prescription management, and direct communication between stakeholders. The platform enhances efficiency, streamlines healthcare workflows, and improves overall patient care.

Technologies: HTML5,CSS,JavaScript,Express.js,MongoDB,GitHub,AWS Elastic Beanstalk
